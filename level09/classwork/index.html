GOALS (Goal Oriented Academics LLC) is a specialized tutoring and academic support organization based in California. It provides services for students from elementary to college levels, focusing on executive functioning coaching, academic tutoring, and supporting neurodiverse learners. They emphasize building skills like organization, time management, and critical thinking. GOALS offers both in-person and remote programs, tailored to individual learning styles and needs. The founder, Phillip Roberts, integrates a neuroscience-based approach into the curriculum, aiming to empower students to become independent learners
